Reproductive health approach endorsed in draft of Cairo document.
A central theme of the Third Session of the Preparatory Committee for the 1994 International Conference on Population and Development was the need to shift from family planning to a more holistic reproductive health approach. Also urged was greater stress on male involvement in family planning programs and men's responsibility for contraception. Representatives of both the Women's Caucus and nongovernmental organizations lobbied to have their positions incorporated into the draft Program of Action. A partnership between governments and nongovernmental organizations is critical to the implementation of proposals adopted at the Cairo conference.
