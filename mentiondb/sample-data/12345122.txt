Family and social networks. First results of a BiB survey.
The author analyzes results from a 1990 survey of family and social networks in the former Federal Republic of Germany. "Our project...focuses on the question as to what extent the changes in the observed demographic factors do affect and modify the system of the family with its various acts of assistance and support (financial help, emotional support, rendering of services)." The questionnaire used is provided, and survey methods are described.
