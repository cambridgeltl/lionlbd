[On January 1, 1994, more than 5% of the total population of the Netherlands were non-Dutch nationals].
"On 1 January 1994 779 thousand non-Dutch nationals had residence in the Netherlands, i.e. 5.1% of the total population." Information is included on nationality of migrants, age and sex distribution, natural growth, net migration, and net changes of nationality. (SUMMARY IN ENG)
